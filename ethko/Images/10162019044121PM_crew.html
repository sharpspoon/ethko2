I was born in Ohio, but—by the grace of God—raised in Alabama. My family moved to Huntsville, via Dallas, TX, when I was three and I have lived most of the rest of my life here in Auburn. Naturally I came to Auburn to study engineering. Eventually I discovered a more suitable major that blended my love of science with that of language and art—graphic design. After one or two excursions into the world beyond, I landed back in Auburn to teach, learn and practice … and raise my beautiful daughter, who—by the grace of God—was born in Alabama.
